Care Plan Assistant

Instructions:

This is one of my first projects I made to help with assessments at the nursing facility I was working at. The purpose of it was to help me save time on writing baseline care plans for patients. This was a very time consuming process before I made this. When a patient comes in, they have to have a care plan that contains all of their diagnoses and medications. It was not uncommon for a patient to have a list of 20-30 diagnoses working with the geriatric population and a large amount of medications. To write a care plan, you would have to go through by hand, manually separate and categorize the diagnoses and medications, then make the care plan and copy everything in. With the help of this program, it shortened the time it took by a significant amount. The way I would use it is by taking the list of diagnoses/medications and copy/paste them into the input box, add it to a category, and once they were all in I would complete the care plan by using the complete button. It would take a few seconds per item
